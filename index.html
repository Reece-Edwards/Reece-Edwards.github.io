Welcome to: Data-Driven

Welcome to the Data-Driven website, a project focused on mapping parts of the open government data provided by the UK government at The .gov website This is a project with a small scope to fit a given time frame.

The top nav-bar will allow you to navigate to the other sections of the website, whether you wish to find out more about how the website is made and the choices made behind it, or if you want to get straight to viewing the data visualization.
