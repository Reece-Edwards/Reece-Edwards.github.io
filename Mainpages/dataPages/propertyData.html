Welcome to the Housing Price Data visualization Page!

These charts represent data taken from The land and property data page of the data.gov website

These following graphs all represent the average house prices in the UK across various regions. each graph starts in 1968, and ends in 2025. As the graph is updated by month looking at individual data points is difficult. These graphs are instead to show a larger trend in housing prices across the Country!

All of these graphs show an upwards trend of house prices that has only gotten steeper over the decades Whilst there have been small dips in prices they don't last long and quickly rise again. With the numerous data points provided it'd be easy to gather conclusions if necessary.

All data is provided by the UK government at The open data project
