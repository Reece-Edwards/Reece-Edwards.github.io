Welcome to the Death Data visualization Page!

These charts represent data taken from The death data page of the data.gov website

The data above represents the total number of deaths in Wales and England, from 1992 - 2024. This dataset contains both female and male death rates but this represents the total combined number.

The following graphs will show the individual male and female death counts.

Deaths Divided By Gender

The data above shows us that whilst initially early in the 90s, the number of female deaths were typically above the death rate of Males, the amount of deaths for females never reached the same peak the male death count reached

The following data represents the UK's ASMR's (Age-standardised mortality rates) rate by region (including wales)

As can be seen above, consistently throughout the regions, males had a higher ASMR rate than females
