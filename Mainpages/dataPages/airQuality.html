Welcome to the air quality Data visualization Page!

These charts represent data taken from The air quality page of the data.gov website

Mean Number of days per urban site when air pollution was moderate or higher in the UK 2010-2024

This chart represents the mean number of days the UK's air pollution as considered "Moderate" or higher with a yearly range of 2010 - 2024, showing a sharp decline after the year 2011, and an even sharper decline in the year 2021, representing the UK lockdown.

This sharp decline shows a great improvement to UK air quality and overall a look into how we're slowly fixing the environment over time.

All data is provided by the UK government at The open data project
