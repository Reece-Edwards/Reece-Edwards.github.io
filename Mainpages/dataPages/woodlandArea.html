Welcome to the Woodland Data visualization Page!

These charts represent data taken from The woodland page of the data.gov website

Total woodland over the years (in thousand hectares)

The data above represents how many thousand hectares of woodland the UK has across its lands over the given years. this data set is provided in graph form by the data.gov website, and is the first overlook into national woodland statistics one can obtain.

Distribution of - woodland : non-woodland

The three graphs above represent the area_hectares of woodland vs non-woodland in the UK from 2022 to 2024. All three datasets were cleaned by me by hand, but all three "uncleaned" datasets in their entirety are provided under the "/Datasets/uncleaned-datasets" folder.

From what we can see in these three graphs, and the graph provided at the top we see a clear trend that the UK as a whole is slowly gaining more woodland area which in the long-run should aid in the fight against climate change.

All data is provided by the UK government at The open data project
